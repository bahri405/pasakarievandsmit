A Wide Range of Legal Services

PRS Legal consists of highly committed and trusted lawyers with more than 15 (fifteen) years of experience in the legal industry, both in law offices, multinational companies, government institutions.

PRS Legal is a full-service law firm in Indonesia with a diverse team of lawyers and network. We provide legal services from upstream to downstream, cooperation from the initiative stage until the closure/termination of the cooperation, with personal, high-quality and professional attention to our clients, and of course with efficient and effective work.
